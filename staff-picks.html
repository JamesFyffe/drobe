LOOKING TO EARN A REWARD?

Every week a lucky user of the robodrobe forums has a chance to win a $50 gift card for having the highest rated outfit!

The rules are simple

1. Each user must make and submit their outfit via the forums before the deadline at the end of the week.

2. Each outfit must contain at least one of the items provided in the staff picks for the week.

3. Each user can only submit one outfit.

Best of luck to all those entering!
